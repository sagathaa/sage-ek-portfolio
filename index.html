The Story

When the social media manager left, I didn't wait to be asked I stepped in. At Pro Lash I took full ownership of their content calendar across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, designing and scheduling 21+ posts a week while keeping the brand voice consistent and fresh.

My biggest contribution was a campaign I pitched from scratch: "Pick a bag, we'll pick your lash." Matching lash styles to birth months a playful, shareable format that became the brand's highest-engagement post in months and sparked a whole series of follow-up content.

I also introduced data tracking into the feed strategy, added product education posts, and elevated the visual quality of shoots bringing intention to every frame.

The Story

At Post Malone's Big Ass Stadium Tour, I looked around and saw a sea of overpriced, forgettable merch. I knew I could do better and more importantly, I knew other fans felt the same way.

So I designed pieces I'd actually wear. Original graphics, quality blanks, a brand identity that felt like it belonged at a show. I handled everything: concept, design, production sourcing, and influencer outreach for launch campaigns.

The result? $1,000+ in revenue within the first two weeks proof that when you design from a place of genuine enthusiasm, people notice.

Slope School is built on a radical idea: that kids can learn twice as fast when the experience is designed around them. As a Guide, I lead 13 learners through purpose-driven projects, individualized growth plans, and a community culture built on curiosity.

This role sits at the intersection of everything I care about experience design, human connection, and the belief that the right environment changes everything.
